# Product Brief: Virtual AI Photo Studio

## Core Concept

Users select a virtual studio, upload 5-10 selfies, and receive a complete photo session in that studio: multiple poses, angles, crops, and expressions with consistent identity.

The product should not feel like a generic AI avatar generator. It should feel like booking a studio session and receiving a curated gallery.

## MVP Offer

**Modern Business Studio**

Professional business portraits for LinkedIn, founders, consultants, managers, and personal brand profiles.

Primary offer:

> Turn 5-10 selfies into 40 professional studio photos.

## User Flow

1. Landing page with one clear offer.
2. Studio page with preview images and shot examples.
3. Selfie upload guide.
4. Photo quality check.
5. Payment.
6. AI generation progress.
7. Gallery with favorites, single downloads, and ZIP download.

## Upload Guide

Ask the user for 8-10 photos:

1. Front-facing neutral expression.
2. Front-facing slight smile.
3. Left three-quarter angle.
4. Right three-quarter angle.
5. Left profile.
6. Right profile.
7. Slightly from above.
8. Slightly from below.
9. Daylight photo.
10. Optional extra photo without glasses or heavy shadows.

## Quality Checks

Before accepting images for generation, check:

- A face is visible.
- The face is not too small.
- The image is not blurry.
- The image is not too dark.
- The user is not wearing sunglasses, mask, or heavy occlusion.
- Images are not near-duplicates.

## MVP Output

Start with:

- 1 studio
- 10 shot templates
- 4 variations per shot
- 40 output images

## Do Not Build Yet

- Mobile app
- Video generation
- Talking avatars
- 100 style options
- Public API
- Team accounts
- Subscription plans
- Multilingual flow

These can come after the first paid users and quality validation.
